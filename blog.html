Technology and its impacts

Sujan Chhetri Poudel

May 28, 2021





Technology is one of the fundamental requirements of our daily lives in this era
It is used in each and every aspects of daily life and has created a technology related lifestyle in people. It plays an important role in society today. Although technology has vastly improved our lives positively, it also has led to many negative consequences.

       Technology has positively affected our daily lives. Through the start of the day to the end, mostly all the daily activities are performed in the presence of technology. It has helped people in various sectors like communication, transportation, education, household works, health and many more. The use of mobile phone for communication, the use of vehicles for transportation, the use of home appliances for household work, the use of different advance machinery in a hospital and many more have made the daily life of every individual comfortable. The use of technology has also upgraded the level of quality education received. Furthermore, the use of internet has boosted each aspects of technology. The technologies as a medium to access each related information on the internet has paved a new way of living where every problem can be solved while casually surfing through the internet. However, with the development of the technologies people have been negatively affected.

       Technological development is one of the major causes of health depreciation. The use of technology has created laziness and dull-mindedness in people, causing multiple health related diseases like diabetes, insomnia and many more. The use of technologies for a long time can cause different health hazard deducting the life span of humans. The complete dependence on technology has reduced the creativeness of a person. Often teens who are highly involved in mobile and other techs are observed to have low creativity and prone to lack social skills. Furthermore, the excessive use of techs has caused psychological impacts like depression and anxiety.

      In a nutshell, technology has been a boon to humans which has created a different technology related lifestyle, however it also creates negative impacts on humans which should be taken in consideration.
